Twinkie Wiener Sandwich

Weird Al's Twinkie Wiener Sandwich

Impress and gross out your friends with this strange delicacy as seen in the movie UHF

- Ingredients -

  • Twinkie
  • Hot dog
  • A can of Cheez Whiz
  • (optional) Milk to dunk it in!

- Steps -

  1. Cut the twinkie longways
  2. Place the hotdog into the twinkie
  3. Smother the wiener in Cheese Whiz
  4. (optional) Dunk it in milk
