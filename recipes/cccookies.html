Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chocolate chip cookies are soft and chewy, with just the right amount of chocolate chips. The perfect treat for any occasion!

- Ingredients -

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

- Steps -

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
